=== About Sustainability Cell IIT Bombay ===

The Sustainability Cell at IIT Bombay is a student-driven initiative dedicated to promoting sustainable practices and environmental consciousness within the campus and beyond. We work towards creating a more sustainable future through various projects, events, and awareness campaigns.

Our mission is to integrate sustainability into every aspect of campus life, from waste management to energy conservation, while fostering a culture of environmental responsibility among students, faculty, and staff.

Key Statistics:
- 20+ Active Members
- 10+ Projects Completed
- 15+ Events Organized
- 1000+ Students Impacted

Core Focus Areas:
1. Innovation - Developing and implementing innovative solutions for sustainability challenges
2. Education - Spreading awareness and knowledge about sustainable practices
3. Community - Building a strong community of sustainability advocates and practitioners

   - Registrations: 500+

   - Launched on 24th January via Unstop

2. Corporate-Oriented Projects (December 2024, Online)
   - Two winter projects: 'Think Like a CSO' and 'Think Like a Green Investor'
   - Focus: Corporate sustainability and green finance
   - Registrations: 300+
   - Offered experiential learning through real-world business scenarios

3. SustAInify (February 2024, Online)
   - Competition to address real-world sustainability issues using machine learning
   - Dates: 10th-11th February
   - Prize: Rs. 30,000 in cash prizes
   - Focus: Data-driven climate solutions

4. Airathon Mumbai 2022 (October 2022, IIT Bombay Campus)
   - Pan India competition to enhance Mumbai's air quality
   - Collaboration with Institute Technical Council, sponsored by Fitizen India
   - Two tracks: Technical and Campaign
   - Prize: Rs. 60,000
   - Submissions: 150+
   - Achievement: Asia's largest and world's second-largest air quality competition

=== Campus Implementations ===

1. EV Buggy Stops
   - Strategic implementation at high-traffic areas including Lecture Hall Complex and student residential zones
   - Purpose: Optimize usage, enhance battery efficiency, and reduce idle time

2. AC Optimization in LHC (Lecture Hall Complex)
   - New doors installed to enhance insulation
   - Purpose: Reduce energy consumption and minimize energy loss
   - Status: Installation completed, additional improvements planned

3. Waste Data Display Boards in Mess
   - Integrated into every hostel
   - Purpose: Track and reduce food wastage
   - Shows transparent view of daily food wastage
   - Engages residents in reducing environmental footprint

4. Tree Plantation (Independence Day 2022)
   - Collaborative initiative with Team Shunya
   - United sustainability-focused groups from institutes across India
   - Purpose: Foster environmental consciousness and contribute to greener future

5. Swacchta Pakhwada 2023 (September 16-30)
   - Supported Government of India's Swachhta Pakhwada initiative
   - Documented and reported hostel cleanliness efforts qualitatively and quantitatively

6. Bin Segregation Project
   - Association with Hostel Affairs Council
   - Waste bin segregation initiative at IIT Bombay
   - Purpose: Efficient waste disposal, recycling, reduce carbon footprint

7. Carbon Footprinting
   - Collaboration with Professor Vikram Vishal (Earth Science) and Professor Arnab Dutta (Chemistry)
   - Carbon footprint assessment of IIT Bombay
   - Emission breakdown: 95% from Scope 2, 4.4% from Scope 1, 0.6% from Scope 3

8. IITB Half Marathon (October 30th)
   - Campaign: #BreatheBetterMumbai
   - Raised awareness about air pollution
   - Partnered with recycling organization for plastic bottle recycling
   - Used waste cycle cardboard boxes for pollution messages

9. Green Cup Launch (November)
   - Collaboration with HA Council
   - Attendees: 100+ students from all hostels
   - Introduced need, benefits, and scoring system of Green Cup initiative
