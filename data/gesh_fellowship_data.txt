GESH IITB Living Lab Fellowship Program - Complete List of Fellowship Holders and Their Names

The GESH IITB Living Lab Fellowship is a unique opportunity for students to work on pre-vetted sustainability projects within the IITB ecosystem. This initiative drives meaningful change on campus and equips students with the skills to become sustainability leaders.
